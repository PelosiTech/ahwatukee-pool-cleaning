About Ahwatukee Pool Cleaning

Your neighbors in Ahwatukee, keeping pools pristine since 2014

Our Story

Jason and Lisa Carter started Ahwatukee Pool Cleaning in 2014 as a husband-and-wife team. After years of frustration with unreliable pool services for their own family pool, they decided to build the kind of company they wished existed — one that actually shows up, communicates clearly, and treats every pool like their own.

What started as a side project serving their Mountain Park Ranch neighbors has grown into Ahwatukee's most trusted pool service. With a team of five full-time technicians, they now care for over 180 family pools across the Foothills.

Why Ahwatukee Trusts Us

We're a family company that understands families. Your kids, your pets, your backyard barbecues — we make sure your pool is always safe, clean, and ready for fun. No corporate runaround, just real people who care.

Our Commitment

We understand that your pool is a significant investment and a centerpiece of outdoor living here in the Valley. Whether you're in a gated community or a custom home, we treat every pool as if it were our own.

  • Family-owned & operated — we live in Ahwatukee too
  • 10+ years keeping Ahwatukee pools swim-ready
  • Licensed, bonded & fully insured
  • Kid & pet-safe chemical practices
  • Friendly, communicative service — we text before every visit
  • Eco-friendly chemical options available

Service Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with any service visit, let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back to make it right — at no additional charge. Period.
